About Myself

My full name is Roel.K.Thomas and I am currently a 16 year old junior in High School. I was born in Brooklyn New York and
lived there for the first eight years of my life before moving to my current home in Mineola New York. I have two loving

honers classes as well as regular required classes. I hope to graduate high school next year and hopefully attend my dream
school of the Uniersity of Michigan in the fall of 2018.
